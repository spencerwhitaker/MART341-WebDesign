Grilled Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly
  • Butter or butter substitute

Directions

  1. Preheat a skillet or pan to a low heat.
  2. Cut off a small cube of butter or butter substitute and let it melt in the preheated pan.
  3. Take two slices of bread. Spread PB on one side of the first slice.
  4. Spread jelly on one side of the other slice.
  5. Put the two slices together
  6. Gently place the sandwich onto the preheated pan or skillet, making sure the melted butter has coated the pan.
  7. Let the sandwich sit for one minute.
  8. Using a non-stick spatula, gently flip the sandwich over, taking care to not let the two slices slide apart.
  9. let the sandwich sit for one more minute.
  10. Remove sandwich from pan, let sit for one minute to cool, then enjoy!
