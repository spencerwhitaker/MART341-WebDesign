  The world wide web is a collection of information that is accessed by the connection of the internet's networks.
2. Briefly describe how the internet works.
  The internet is composed of various networks. To access this, one can use a Local Area Network (LAN) or an Internet Service Provider (ISP). 
  Messages are sent between different parts of the network through packets that are packed, transported, and unpacked following certain protocols. 
  The networks are built through several levels and include many components, including modems, routers, and physical connections to servers.
