About

Meet Chris, our Head Barber here at the Men's Hair Salon. Chris is a lifelong barber, having grown up giving haircuts to his siblings and friends. Chris draws inspiration from the classical years in America's history, but loves nothing more than to connect his skills to your vision and make you look the best version of yourself. The type of guy to grab a beer with you and catch the game, Chris is the perfect man for the job.
